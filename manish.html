e - DAM is a student-based club of IARE that aims to build a technically well-equipped group with the most resourceful digital skills required in the modern workplace. To create and develop websites, applications and designing.

About us

e - DAM (e - Designing and marketing) is a neoteric entity which focusses on the most in-demand domains of designing and marketing. We are a student-based club organized by Institute of Aeronautical Engineering, Dundigal. Our motive is to garner seasoned expertise and provide services to help small scale businesses by building them websites and/or apps. Our team comprises of hardworking tech, design and marketing enthusiasts who strongly believe in your vision, our design..
