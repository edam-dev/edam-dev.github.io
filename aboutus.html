ABOUT US



“The art challenges the technology, and
the technology inspires the art.”

E-DAM is an abbreviated entity which focusses on few
the most in-demand fields-designing and marketing.
We are a student-based club organised by Institute of Aeronautical Engineering, Dundigal.
Our motive is to help small scale businesses by building them
websites and/or apps, introducing them to e-commerce world,
providing young talented developers/designers a platform to
showcase their skills. Our team comprises of
hardworking tech, design and marketing enthusiasts who strongly
believe in your vision, our design.
